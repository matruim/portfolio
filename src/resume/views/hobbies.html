Games

Pen and Paper RPG's

I started playing pen and paper role playing games as a young man. I have many a fond memory with my brothers sitting around the dinner table late into the night playing a character of my own creation. I love playing the Palladium Games System. This is the one that started it all for me. From Palladium, Rifts, TMNT, After the Bomb, Phase World, Heros Unlimited and more I love this game system and the creativity that you can express in its rule system. I have also played Dungeons and Dragons , and Pathfinder. As well as a few less popular game systems. I also have started to teach my own children how to play pen and paper RPG games. One of my children struggles with reading and it was an easy way to get them to read and do math all while having fun.

Video Games

Oddly enough I am not the video game freak at my house. I love them and am fond of classic NES and SNES JRPG games. However video games are more often played by my wife and kids. I play them and enjoy the time I spend on them with my family, but the games I love to play suck to much of my life away and so I dont play them often.

Church

My Religion is part of my everyday life. I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Because God is important to me and has been my whole life I decided to serve a proselyting mission. I was assigned to serve the people of Guatemala and have a love for the beauty of that country and those people. I also have plans in the future once my children are grown to serve another mission together with my wife. I also speak with my children about my mission and the role that it played in shaping my future.
